Reading map

What matters in a good FAQ

  • Start from user intent: before booking, before arrival, during the stay, and after checkout.
  • A good question is specific to the page or property, not a generic heading like “Amenities”.
  • A good answer is short, verifiable, and clearly marks facts that still need a reliable source.
  • Categories are internal guidance for the model. They should not turn into generic questions.
